What Is a Genetic Algorithm?

▼

A genetic algorithm is a problem-solving method inspired by how natural selection works.

Imagine trying to find the best solution to a difficult problem: instead of testing every possibility, this method creates a group of possible solutions (like a population of individuals).

These solutions are then combined and slightly changed, similar to how parents pass on traits to their children and how small mutations happen in nature.

Over time, the “fittest” solutions survive and improve, getting closer and closer to the ideal answer.
